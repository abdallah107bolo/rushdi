To My Dearest Friend Rushdi

In the tapestry of life, friendship is the golden thread that binds our hearts together. Mohammed, whom we all lovingly know as Rushdi, you have brought so much joy, laughter, and warmth into my life. Your presence has been a blessing, like an oasis in the desert, refreshing and life-giving.

As you celebrate another year of life, I want you to know how grateful I am to have you as my friend. Your kindness, your wisdom, and your unwavering support have made my life richer in countless ways.

May Allah bless you with happiness, success, and prosperity in all your endeavors. May this year bring you even more reasons to smile and may our friendship continue to grow stronger with each passing day.
